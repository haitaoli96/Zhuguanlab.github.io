About Us

The Zhu Guan Lab at the Institute of Zoonosis, Jilin University, focuses on zoonotic protozoan parasites such as Cryptosporidium, Giardia and coccidia. We combine parasitology, biochemistry, genomics, and drug discovery to understand host–parasite interaction, cross-species transmission and to develop new diagnostics and therapeutics for zoonotic diseases.
